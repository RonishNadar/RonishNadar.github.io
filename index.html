Ronish Nadar

Robotics engineer pursuing an M.S. in Mechatronics & Robotics at NYU, with experience building autonomous systems that combine perception, planning, and control. I enjoy taking robots from idea to working end-to-end hardware/software integration.

Key skills

  • Robotics & Controls: SLAM, path planning (A*, D*, RRT/RRT*), navigation stacks; PID, LQR, MPC; Pure Pursuit; system identification
  • AI & Perception: OpenCV, YOLO, ArUco/AprilTag tracking; Vision-Language Models (Moondream); LLMs (Gemini, Llama); RAG; Transformers
  • Embedded & Electronics: STM32, ESP32, Raspberry Pi, NVIDIA Jetson; UART/I2C/SPI/CAN; Wi-Fi/BLE/LoRa; PCB design (Altium, KiCad, Eagle)
  • Tools: ROS/ROS2, Python/C++, Linux (Ubuntu), Git, Docker, PyTorch, Simulink
